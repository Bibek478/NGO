Mission Nirmal Bangla

Supporting Swachh Bharat Mission for Rural Sanitation

About the Project

Mission Nirmal Bangla, aligned with Swachh Bharat Mission, aims to promote cleanliness, hygiene, and construction of toilets to end open defecation. PSRWS actively works in Phansidewa Block of Darjeeling District as a key implementation partner.

Key Activities

  • Community meetings and house-to-house awareness visits
  • Technical support for toilet construction and monitoring
  • Facilitating subsidy access under SBM guidelines
  • Promoting behavioral change and hygiene practices
  • Supporting villages in achieving ODF status
